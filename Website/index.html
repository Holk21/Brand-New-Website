About

Welcome to Auckland Nation Wide RP, a New Zealand-based FiveM roleplay community built on realism, fairness, and fun. We offer professional police, fire, and medical roleplay with active staff and a welcoming environment.

Departments

  • Police Department: Enforce laws and protect citizens.
  • Fire Department: Respond to emergencies and save lives.
  • EMS: Provide medical care in critical situations.
  • Civilian Operations: Build unique character stories and contribute to RP.

Rules

  1. Respect all players and staff.
  2. No RDM/VDM.
  3. No meta or power gaming.
  4. Use realistic RP behavior at all times.
  5. Follow staff instructions when given.
